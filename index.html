Sunrise Animation

Project Description: This interactive WebGL application simulates a realistic sunrise, transitioning smoothly from night to dawn. Users can interact directly with the shader's parameters through an embedded text editor, making real-time adjustments to the animation's effects.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Text Editor: Adjust shader code in real-time to see immediate changes in the animation.
  • Delayed Start: The sunrise does not begin immediately, mimicking the gradual approach of dawn.
  • Fade-In Effects: The sunrise gradually brightens, simulating the natural increase in light at dawn.
  • Vertical Ascent: The sun visually ascends over the horizon, enhancing the realism of the sunrise.
  • Color Transitions: The background dynamically changes from the dark hues of night to the warm tones of sunrise and into the brighter blues of day.
  • Atmospheric Effects: Simulates atmospheric scattering to add depth and realism to the sunrise.
